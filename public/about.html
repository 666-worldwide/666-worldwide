About the Circle

Harvesting fortunes, Hidden in plain sight.

666 WORLDWIDE represents an enduring circle that operates on a global scale, aiming for the accumulation of wealth through member alignment.

Structure

How the circle is organized.

Membership is administered centrally. Agents recruit and vouch; the registry issues numbers and keeps the record. Nothing about your standing depends on who you know inside the group — your number is proof enough.

Standards

What we ask of members.

Accuracy in your profile, discretion about the circle's internal matters, and good standing with your agent. In return, membership is permanent and your record is never reissued to anyone else.
